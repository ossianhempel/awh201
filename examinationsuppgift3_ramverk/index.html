FAQ

Electric cars are powered solely by electricity, whereas hybrid cars combine a gasoline or diesel engine with an electric motor.

Charging times for electric cars vary based on the type of charger and the car's battery capacity. Typically, it can range from 30 minutes to 12 hours.

Electric cars can have a higher upfront cost, but they often have lower operational costs due to savings on fuel and maintenance.
